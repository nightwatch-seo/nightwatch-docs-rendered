Step 1: Adding Website

What are AI Tags, and what is the benefit of applying them?

AI Tags are a powerful tool in search engine optimization. The four primary intent categories are:

  • Informational,

  • Navigational,

  • Commercial, and

  • Transactional.

When you have your keywords listed, you can click the Apply AI Tags button, and Nightwatch will automatically and efficiently categorize your keywords based on the specific purpose or goal behind a user's search query.

This categorization process enables you to analyze keywords according to their intent, providing valuable insights for your strategy. Each keyword's assigned intent type can reveal whether a searcher is in the early stages of information gathering, seeking a particular website, exploring brands and products, or actively looking to perform a transaction, such as making a purchase.

By utilizing intent tags, you can tailor your content and marketing efforts to better align with your audience's intentions, ultimately improving your online visibility and engagement.
